About HotelSys

HotelSys is a machine learning powered web application designed to predict the likelihood of customer cancellation of hotel reservations. It enables hotel management and customers to make informed decisions based on historical data and advanced predictive models.

  • Easy-to-use web interface with responsive design.
  • Accurate prediction model trained on real reservation data.
  • Designed with a minimalistic, professional look for usability.
